COOKIES

Cookies are one of my favourite, here is a recipe of delicious cookies.

INGREDIENTS

  • Flour : 1 cup
  • Ghee : 1 cup
  • Sugar : 1 tsp
  • Vanilla Essence : 1 tsp
  • Salt : a pinch
  • Baking soda : 1 tsp
  • Egg : 1
  • Cocopowder : 1 tsp

METHOD

  1. Pre heat the owen at 100 degree.
  2. In a bowl add plain flour, sugar, vanilla essence, egg yolk and ghee.
  3. mix well untill a smooth dough is formed.
  4. Roll a dough with rolling pin into the shape of chappati.
  5. cut round with the help of cutter.
